How To Summon and Customize Mannequins

This simple tutorials gives you all the methods that you can do to summon and customize an Mannequin in Minecraft!

Supported Versions:

1.21.9+

Tutorial Steps:

  1. You may take any of these commands with the nbt components and combine them
  2. Just make sure you put commas and components in the right place!

Commands by Row:

Row 1

1. [impulse] /summon minecraft:mannequin ~ ~1 ~

Row 2

2. [impulse] /summon minecraft:mannequin ~ ~1 ~ {profile:"Vivtorsing",CustomName:"Subscribe"}

Row 3

3. [impulse] /summon minecraft:mannequin ~ ~1 ~ {profile:"Vivtorsing",CustomName:"Subscribe",hide_description:true}
4. [chain] /summon minecraft:mannequin ~ ~1 ~ {profile:"Vivtorsing",CustomName:"Subscribe",description:"To Me!"}

Row 4

5. [impulse] /summon minecraft:mannequin ~ ~1 ~ {profile:"Notch",main_hand:"left",immovable:true}

Row 5

6. [impulse] /summon minecraft:mannequin ~ ~1 ~ {profile:{name:"Flowers",cape:"block/diamond_block"}}
7. [chain] /summon minecraft:mannequin ~ ~1 ~ {profile:{texture:"block/diamond_block",cape:"block/netherite_block"}}
8. [chain] /summon minecraft:mannequin ~ ~1 ~ {profile:{texture:"block/diamond_block",cape:"block/netherite_block",elytra:"block/gold_block",model:"wide"}}

Row 6

9. [impulse] /summon minecraft:mannequin ~ ~1 ~ {profile:"Vivtorsing",hidden_layers:["hat","cape"]}

Row 7

10. [impulse] /summon minecraft:mannequin ~ ~1 ~ {profile:"Jeb_",pose:"crouching"}
11. [chain] /summon minecraft:mannequin ~ ~1 ~ {profile:"Jeb_",pose:"swimming"}
12. [chain] /summon minecraft:mannequin ~ ~1 ~ {profile:"Jeb_",pose:"fall_flying"}
13. [chain] /summon minecraft:mannequin ~ ~1 ~ {profile:"Jeb_",pose:"sleeping"}
